In the vibrant city of Bremen, Patricio, a 20-year-old Mexican CS major,
begins a new chapter of his life. Having in the city a newly made friend Mark from his Math 1 course, he seems to be adjusting to the rhythms of university
and the lively atmosphere of the city, he also finds himself drawn to Emma, a
fellow student whose presence adds a spark to his days. As he navigates the
challenges of studying abroad, Patricio's thoughts often gravitate towards Emma,
hinting at the possibility of a budding romance amidst the backdrop of his fresh start.
